1. Where do we come from?

90's - 2000's

Sir Tim Berners-Lee created the first WebPage: http://info.cern.ch/hypertext/WWW/TheProject.html

... and it was responsive!

WWW became popular

  • Javascript was used to validate formulars
  • Layouts were made with tables
  • Websites were static (except for blink!)
  • Animations were done with gifs

Mobile Gaming

Native development was the preferred way to deliver games to mobile

Flash reign

Whenever some dynamism was needed, Flash was used:

  • Video Players
  • Games
  • Even Websites!

HTML5 begins his journey

It's late 2000's and HTML5 makes their first steps. However, it was not yet seen as a possible successor of Flash

But then...
